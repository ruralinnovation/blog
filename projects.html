Projects

Research projects combining multiple datasets and tools

Rural Economic Outlook

Comprehensive analysis of rural economic trends using 12 federal datasets. Examines employment, population, business formation, and migration patterns.

Datasets: QCEW, ACS, Census PEP, BEA GDP, BDS, BFS, Building Permits, IRS Migration, USPTO Patents, and more

Broadband Equity Analysis

Analysis of broadband access disparities and their impact on rural communities.

Datasets: FCC Broadband, ACS, Census PEP
