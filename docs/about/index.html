About Rural Dataverse

Our Mission

Rural Dataverse is the data and research hub for the Mapping & Data Analytics team at the Center on Rural Innovation (CORI). We provide access to curated datasets, R packages, research projects, and analytical insights focused on rural America.

What We Offer

  • Datasets: Curated data on broadband, economic development, demographics, and more
  • R Packages: Tools for accessing and analyzing rural data
  • Projects: Research and analysis projects with full methodology
  • Blog: Technical posts on data analysis, visualization, and methods
  • Resources: Guides, crosswalks, and reference materials

The Team

The Mapping & Data Analytics team at CORI combines expertise in data science, geographic analysis, economics, and policy research to support rural innovation and economic development initiatives.

Get In Touch

Questions about our data or tools? Want to collaborate? Visit the CORI website or check out our work on GitHub.
