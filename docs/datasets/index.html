Datasets

Federal and public datasets for rural innovation research

QCEW Employment & Wages

Quarterly Census of Employment and Wages - detailed employment and wage data by industry and geography.

American Community Survey

Comprehensive demographic and socioeconomic data from the U.S. Census Bureau.

FCC Broadband Data

Broadband availability and deployment data from the Federal Communications Commission.

Census Population Estimates

Annual population estimates by county from the U.S. Census Bureau.

BEA Real GDP

County-level real GDP data from the Bureau of Economic Analysis.

Business Dynamics Statistics

Establishment and firm dynamics data from the U.S. Census Bureau.

Business Formation Statistics

High-frequency business formation data from the U.S. Census Bureau.

Building Permits

Monthly building permit data from the U.S. Census Bureau.

Population Components

Components of population change including births, deaths, and migration.

IRS Migration Data

County-to-county migration flows from IRS tax return data.

USPTO Patents

Patent data from the U.S. Patent and Trademark Office.

USDA County Typology

County classifications based on economic and demographic characteristics.
